Projects: Hey, Doc.

Role: UX/UI

Duration: 2 Months

Overview.

Hey, Doc is a mobile app designed to make booking medical appointments easy and improve communication between patients and healthcare providers. Targeting all age groups and demographics across South Africa, this app aims to reduce the hassle and stress of visiting the doctor and cut down on wait times at medical centers.

1. Problem Statement.

The challenge is to make healthcare accessible by simplifying appointment bookings, reducing wait times, and enhancing patient-provider communication for all demographics.

2. Lo-Fi Wireframe.

A low-fidelity wireframe was created to get user feedback before moving on to other step of design

3. Data Collection.

The wireframe created was presented to a few users to test satisfaction rate and for feedback. The criticism was constructive and helped us to understand the different perspectives

5. Key Takeaways.
